Privacy Policy for IslamicHub Pro

Last updated: June 2026

IslamicHub Pro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

IslamicHub Pro is designed to work primarily offline. The app may access:

  • Location data (optional) — Used only to calculate accurate Qibla direction and prayer times based on your city selection. This is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted to any server.
  • Local storage — Your app preferences, tasbeeh counts, prayer tracking, fasting records, and other personal data are stored locally on your device only.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or contacts. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics services that track you.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the Aladhan Prayer Times API to fetch accurate prayer timings based on the city you select. No personal data is sent to this service beyond the city name.

Data Storage

All your personal data (tasbeeh counts, deeds tracker, fasting log, sleep tracker, water reminder, etc.) is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data is not transmitted to any external server and remains private to you.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
